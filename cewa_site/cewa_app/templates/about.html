Meet the Team

Here at Cleaning Equipment WA we strive to deliver quality and affordable machines to our valuable customers. Our point of difference is our care and direct involvement with our clients, by this we are referring to us taking calls and us repairing the machines directly, should they need repairing. This is our point of difference.

Brett Thompson

Brett Thompson has a wealth of over 30 years experience in the cleaning equipment machinery. Brett has worked as a service manager for Campbells Janitor Supplies and Statewide Cleaning Supplies and was the founder of Commercial Cleaning Equipment. Cleaning Equipment WA, is specialising in a brand of equipment that has price, quality and performance in mind. We also know that any machine is only as good as the back up service that is available to you, the customer. With that in mind Cleaning Equipment WA will endeavour to keep most parts here in WA to keep you moving when you need it.
